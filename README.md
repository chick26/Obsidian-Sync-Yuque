## ✂️ How to Use

- 🖍 Obtain Your `Token` From [yueque.com](https://www.yuque.com/settings/tokens/new?language=en-us)
  - At least full control of documents | repositories
- 📝 Setup Your `Token` In the Plugin
- 🔐 Login to Obtain Your Account Info.
- ❤️ Enjoy the Plugin

## ✂️ 如何使用
